# Context Engine Contributor License Agreement — STUB

> **Status: placeholder.** The CLA text is being drafted with counsel and does not exist yet.
> Until it exists and a signing flow is in place, **no external contribution can be merged**
> (see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)).

When finalized, the Context Engine CLA will require, at minimum:

- **Copyright assignment** (or an exclusive, unrestricted, sublicensable grant) of the
  contribution to the project owner — full ownership is what keeps the Context Engine EULA
  enforceable while the source stays publicly visible.
- A statement that the contributor has the right to make the assignment (original work, no
  conflicting employer or third-party claims).
- A patent license covering the contribution.

A **Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) sign-off is not sufficient** and will not be accepted
as a substitute.
