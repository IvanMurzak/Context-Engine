1. **Source-available.** The Context Game Engine source code is made visible for reading,
   building, and use under this EULA. It is **not** open source.
2. **Grant (games only).** You may use, modify, and build the engine to develop, build, and
   ship **games** made with the engine, and distribute the engine only as embedded in such a
   shipped game.
3. **Forbidden.** You may not redistribute the engine itself (outside of a shipped game), and
   you may not create, distribute, or commercially exploit a derived or competing game engine,
   middleware, or engine-like product based on this source.
4. **Royalty.** A **2% royalty** applies to **gross revenue per product above USD $200,000 per
   year**. The threshold is annual (resets each year) and **marginal** — the royalty applies
   only to revenue above the threshold. Below the threshold the engine is free: no subscription
   and no AI usage required.
5. **Waiver.** The royalty is **waived** for games developed under an active
   [ai-game.dev](https://ai-game.dev) subscription.
6. **Terms locked per engine version.** The terms that apply to a given engine version never
   change retroactively; new terms apply only to new engine versions.
7. **Contributions.** External contributions require a Contributor License Agreement with
   copyright assignment (see CLA.md); a Developer Certificate of Origin alone is insufficient.
8. **No warranty.** The engine is provided "AS IS", without warranty of any kind, express or
   implied. In no event shall the authors be liable for any claim, damages, or other liability
   arising from its use.
